Tom Odhiambo
Surveyor
Mr Tom Odhiambo is the founder of Maptech Surveyors,a licensed land surveyor based in Kenya.Mr Odhiambo studied in Technical University of Kenya.He has over 30 years experience in land matters.He has done major works in Muranga,Kitengela,Kirinyaga,Magadi,Kajiado,Machakos,Garissa,Isiolo and Nairobi.He has skills in land subdividing,land planning,boundary allinment and general surveying.
