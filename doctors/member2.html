Lewis Omondi
Surveyor
Mr Lewis Omondi is a professional land surveyor based in Kenya.Mr Lewis studied land surveying in Technical University of Kenya where he later studied geospatial science and surveying technology.He has over 10 years experience in land matters.He has done major works in Muranga,Kitengela,Kirinyaga,Magadi,Kajiado,Machakos and Nairobi.He has skills in road surveying,land subdividing,land planning,boundary allinment and aerial view surveying.
